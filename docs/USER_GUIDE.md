## Understanding the Data

### What Makes a Stock "Qualifying"?

A stock qualifies when **ALL** three conditions are met:

1. ‚úÖ **Price Movement ‚â•1%** (up or down)
2. ‚úÖ **OI Change ‚â•10%** (Open Interest surge)
3. ‚úÖ **Breakout/Breakdown** (52-week high/low or significant move)

### Data Types

#### Snapshot Data (Frozen at 9:30 AM)
- **When**: Captured daily at 9:30 AM IST
- **Why**: Freezes the opening momentum
- **Use**: Plan your day trades based on opening strength

#### Live Data (During Market Hours)
- **When**: Real-time during 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST
- **Why**: Track evolving momentum
- **Use**: Monitor intraday changes

### Key Metrics Explained

#### Open Interest (OI)
- **What**: Number of outstanding derivative contracts
- **High OI + Price Move**: Strong institutional interest
- **Rising OI**: New positions being built
- **Falling OI**: Positions being closed

#### Price Change %
- **‚â•1%**: Minimum threshold for qualification
- **‚â•2%**: Strong momentum
- **‚â•3%**: Very strong momentum (higher risk/reward)

#### Direction
- **UP (Bullish)**: Price increasing with OI surge
- **DOWN (Bearish)**: Price decreasing with OI surge
- **NEUTRAL**: No significant movement

1. Navigate to **Alerts** in the header
2. Click **"+ New Alert"**

## Best Practices

### ‚è∞ Timing is Everything

**Before 9:30 AM**
- Review demo dashboard to understand functionality
- Set up alerts for sectors/stocks you're watching
- Prepare your watchlist

**At 9:30 AM (Snapshot Time)**
- Check snapshot data immediately
- Review qualifying sectors and stocks
- Plan your trades based on opening momentum

**9:30 AM - 11:00 AM (Prime Time)**
- Monitor live data for continuation or reversal
- Execute trades based on your strategy
- Use stock detail pages for deeper analysis

**After 11:00 AM**
- Momentum may fade
- Focus on managing open positions
- Less ideal for new entries

### üìà Trading Tips

1. **Don't chase every signal**
   - Focus on 2-3 high-conviction setups
   - Quality over quantity

2. **Combine with technical analysis**
   - Use EdgeUp for momentum screening
   - Confirm with your own chart analysis
   - Check support/resistance levels

3. **Risk management is key**
   - Set stop losses
   - Position size appropriately
   - Don't risk more than 1-2% per trade

4. **Sector rotation matters**
   - If IT is strong, other IT stocks may follow
   - Sector momentum can last multiple days
   - Use historical data to confirm trends

### üéØ How to Use Stock Detail Page

1. Click on any qualifying stock
2. Review:
   - **Price Chart**: Candlestick chart with recent price action
   - **OI Chart**: Open Interest trend (rising OI = strong conviction)
   - **Key Metrics**: Volume, LTP, change%, OI change%
3. Look for:
   - Consistent OI increase over multiple candles
   - Price breaking key levels (52-week high/low)
   - High volume confirmation

## FAQ

### Q: What time does the snapshot get created?
**A:** Every trading day at 9:30 AM IST. This captures the opening market momentum.

### Q: Can I trade based on snapshot data after 9:30 AM?
**A:** The snapshot shows opening momentum. For later trades, use the **Live Monitor** for real-time data.

### Q: Why do some stocks show in watchlist instead of qualifying?
**A:** Watchlist stocks meet the price criteria (‚â•1%) but haven't met the OI threshold (‚â•10%) yet. They're worth monitoring.

### Q: How often does live data refresh?
**A:** Live data refreshes every 30 seconds during market hours (9:15 AM - 3:30 PM IST).

### Q: What happens on market holidays?
**A:** The system recognizes NSE holidays. A banner will show "Market Closed" with the next opening date.

### Q: Can I export the qualifying stocks list?
**A:** Export feature is coming soon! For now, use the stock detail pages to analyze individual stocks.

### Q: How do I interpret high OI with price drop?
**A:** Rising OI + Falling Price = Bearish sentiment (short buildup). This can indicate further downside or a potential short squeeze.

### Q: What's the difference between Dashboard and Live Monitor?
**A:** 
- **Dashboard**: Shows frozen 9:30 AM snapshot (best for planning)
- **Live Monitor**: Shows real-time data (best for monitoring)

### Q: Can I set alerts for specific price levels?
**A:** Currently, alerts are based on sectors qualifying or stocks breaking out. Custom price alerts are planned for future releases.

### Q: How accurate is the data?
**A:** Data comes directly from Dhan API with 1-second rate limiting and 3-retry logic for reliability. Historical snapshots are permanent records.
